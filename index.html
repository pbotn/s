How the bot grows your club’s rake

The goal is simple: keep more tables running for longer, so your room generates more rake from the same player base.

  • • Fills seats when there are not enough players to start or continue a game.
  • • Maintains minimum table activity during night hours and slow weekdays.
  • • Helps reach a stable number of hands per hour on key limits.
  • • Lets your regular players always find a live table instead of leaving the app.
More table activity = more rake for the club.
The bot doesn’t replace your players — it keeps games alive so every visiting player can sit down and generate rake.

What the bot can do

You stay in control while the bot quietly supports your ecosystem in the background.

1

Autonomous seat filling

The bot sits down on selected tables according to your rules: limits, formats, time windows and maximum number of bots per table.

2

Flexible behaviour profiles

Different play styles for different games: tighter or looser, passive or more active — always within the boundaries you set.

3

Simple admin control

Admins decide where and when the bot is allowed to play. You can enable or disable it per table in a couple of clicks.

4

Risk‑aware configuration

Set limits on stakes, table count and playing time so the bot behaves exactly as you want and stays within your risk profile.

Who it is built for

The bot is aimed at clubs and rooms that want more stable rake from their existing traffic.

  • • Private online clubs with fluctuating traffic.
  • • Home-game style rooms that need games during off‑peak hours.
  • • Communities where players often see empty tables and leave.
