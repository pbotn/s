What the bot does for your club

The Controllable Poker Bot is built to support your ecosystem: keep tables active, stabilize rake, and give you a clear picture of how players behave — without handing control to third‑party networks.

A

Autonomous gameplay support

Keeps tables running during low-traffic hours, maintains healthy occupancy, and follows configurable scenarios tailored to your limits and formats.

R

Smart risk‑management

Apply betting and time limits, monitor abnormal patterns, and detect suspicious activity early to reduce potential abuse and collusion risks.

P

Player behavioral analytics

Track styles, activity, and table impact for each player. Access session reports, peak hours, and retention metrics to inform marketing and VIP decisions.

C

Fully controlled by admins

All configuration stays inside your club. Administrators decide where, how, and when bots sit down, and can enable or disable scenarios instantly per table.
